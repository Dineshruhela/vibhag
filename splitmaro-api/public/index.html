Frequently Asked Questions

Is Splitmaro free to use?
Yes! The core features of Splitmaro are completely free. You can split bills, create groups, settle debts, and manage transactions without paying anything. There are no ads or hidden charges.
How does the instant UPI Settlement work?
When you tap 'Settle' on a debt, Splitmaro reads the payee's UPI ID and generates a secure UPI deep-link containing the exact payment amount. This link launches your native UPI app (like GPay, PhonePe, or Paytm) on your phone directly, so you don't have to copy-paste amounts or enter phone numbers.
Can I use Splitmaro offline?
Absolutely. Splitmaro has an offline-first design. All actions you perform are immediately written to your local database cache. Once your internet connection is active, Splitmaro runs a background sync to securely back up and reconcile all transactions.
Is there a monthly subscription for Pro?
No! Splitmaro Pro is a one-time purchase of ₹499. Once you upgrade, you gain lifetime access to all premium features, including unlimited groups, recurring expenses, cloud sync, and budget alerts on all your devices.
