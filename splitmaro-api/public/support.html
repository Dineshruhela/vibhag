Frequently Asked Questions

Is Splitmaro completely free?
Yes, all core splitting and expense ledger features are completely free on iOS. You can sync unlimited transactions in real-time.

How do I request account deletion?
You can wipe all your account data directly inside the mobile app's Profile screen by clicking "Delete Account". Alternatively, you can email us to request server-side purging.
