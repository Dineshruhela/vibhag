# Privacy Policy for Splitmaro

**Last updated:** May 8, 2026

## 1. Introduction
Welcome to Splitmaro. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application ("Splitmaro").

## 2. Information We Collect
Splitmaro is designed with privacy in mind. 
* **Local Storage:** All your data, including groups, friends, expenses, and balances, is stored locally on your device using an SQLite database. 
* **No Cloud Syncing:** We do not transmit your personal financial data to our servers.
* **No Account Required:** You do not need to create an account, provide an email address, or link a phone number to use the core features of the app.

## 3. How We Use Your Information
Because your data is stored locally on your device, we do not access, collect, or use your personal financial information. The data entered into Splitmaro is used solely to provide the application's expense tracking and splitting functionality to you.

## 4. Third-Party Services
We may use third-party services (such as Expo and React Native) to build and run the application. These services may collect basic diagnostic or usage data (such as crash reports) according to their respective privacy policies. 

## 5. Data Security
Your data is stored securely in the app's local sandbox on your device. We recommend securing your device with a passcode or biometric authentication to protect your information. If you uninstall the app, all local data will be permanently deleted.

## 6. Changes to This Privacy Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
